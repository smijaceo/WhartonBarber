## Design Direction

### DO THIS
- **Be UNIQUE** — this should look nothing like a typical barbershop site
- **Gallery is the star** — showcase cuts, fades, transformations. Make it stunning.
- **Clean, premium, masculine but welcoming** vibe
- **Mobile-first** — most barber clients browse on phones
- **Clear CTAs** — "Book Now" buttons linking to Booksy everywhere
- **Fast** — lightweight, no heavy libraries
- **Eye-catching animations** — tasteful scroll reveals, hover effects on gallery, smooth transitions
- Use placeholder images with descriptive alt text (use https://placehold.co/ URLs). Real photos will be swapped in later.
- Show the 5.0 rating and review count prominently — social proof converts

### DO NOT DO THIS
- No dark-mode-with-gold-accents cliché (every barbershop does this)
- No barber pole graphics or scissors clip art
- No generic stock photography aesthetic
- No Inter, Roboto, Arial, Playfair Display, Old Standard fonts
- No cookie-cutter template vibes
- No booking calendar or appointment system — just link to Booksy
- No frameworks (React, Vue, etc.)
- No build steps

## Technical Requirements
