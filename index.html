The Single Chair Philosophy

Richard "Ricky" Wharton made a radical choice when he opened his shop: one chair, one focus, one client at a time.

After years perfecting his craft at Dennison Dahlman, Ricky realized that true excellence comes from undivided attention. Every cut, every conversation, every detail matters.

The result? Clients like Shea who've been coming for over 5 years, following Ricky because they understand the difference between getting a haircut and experiencing craftsmanship.
