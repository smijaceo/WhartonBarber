The Craftsman's Story

Richard "Ricky" Wharton didn't just open another barbershop. After years perfecting his craft at Dennison Dahlman, he created something special: a single-chair experience where quality trumps quantity.

Inspired by Victorian-era craftsmanship and traditional barbering techniques, Ricky combines old-school attention to detail with modern precision. Every cut includes his signature straight razor finishing.

The result? A loyal following of clients who understand the difference between getting a haircut and experiencing true craftsmanship. Some, like Shea, have been coming for over 5 years.
