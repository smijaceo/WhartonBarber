The Single Chair Experience

Richard "Ricky" Wharton made a deliberate choice when he opened his shop: one chair. While others chase volume, he chose focus. While others rush, he takes time.

After years perfecting his craft at Dennison Dahlman, Ricky opened Wharton Barbershop with a simple philosophy: every client deserves undivided attention and uncompromising quality.

The result is a loyal following of clients who understand the difference between getting a haircut and experiencing true craftsmanship.
